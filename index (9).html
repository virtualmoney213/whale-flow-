Rule 2 — 25% Premium Decay

💰 Profit Taking Rules
At +50% profit
SELL HALF
Lock in profit. Let remaining contracts ride for free. You cannot lose money on a trade after this.
At +100% profit
SELL REST
OR trail stop to breakeven and let it run. One 100% winner covers 4 full losses.
