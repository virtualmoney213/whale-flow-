🧲 OI Magnet — Key Guide
Open Interest (OI) = how many contracts are HELD overnight. Think of it like a hotel — OI is how many rooms are occupied tonight.

Volume = how many contracts traded today. Could be opened or closed.

Vol/OI 10×+ = 10 times more volume than existing positions. Someone opened a big NEW bet.
